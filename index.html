About the Project

DEEP-REST is a research project funded through the 2020-2021 Biodiversa and Water JPI joint call, under the BiodivRestore ERA-NET Cofund.

Our project focuses on the geopolitical and environmental aspects of deep-sea ecosystems, particularly in the context of seabed mining. Through advanced visualization tools and comprehensive data analysis, we address critical issues in marine conservation, international maritime law, and sustainable resource management.

Led by the Department of Human Geography at the University of Seville, this initiative combines geographic information systems, political analysis, and environmental research to support informed decision-making in ocean governance.
