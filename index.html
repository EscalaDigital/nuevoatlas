About the Project

DEEP-REST is a research project funded through the 2020-2021 Biodiversa and Water JPI joint call, under the BiodivRestore ERA-NET Cofund.

The purpose of the 'Geopolitical atlas' is to compile information about the territorial dimension of the political-jurisdictional, administrative, and socio-economic aspects of the study sites of the Deep-Rest project to support the development of geopolitical scenarios. Maps, statistical data and other complementary information are structured in the conventional atlas format and as a map viewer
